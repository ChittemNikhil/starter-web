Religious Disabilities Act a) For Indian soldiers, the belief was that if they crossed the ocean, they would lose their religion. They would thus be socially ostracized.
b) Thus, Hindu soldiers skipped appointments that involved serving in a foreign land.
c) But, after the passing of the “Religious Disabilities Act”, they would have to compulsorily take the appointment, otherwise, they would lose their job.
d) At the same time, the use of religious symbols in the military were also restricted.
